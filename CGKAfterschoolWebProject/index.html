Our Method

"Real Global Kids" with Self-expression, Acute Cognitive Ability and a Spirit of Inquiry

The Ability to express yourself to others

Rather than surface language skills, we place importance on the ability to understand other people's ideas, conveying their own ideas and engaging in dialogue. They will also give presentations and hold discussions in order to them develop necessary English skills.

The ability to create without convention

Through the understanding and tolerance of diversity and the use of critical thinking skills to think outside the box, children can develop the ability to find new paths on their own, while still being conscious of the need for both cooperation and independence.

Endless Motivation Toward the Future

Through various activities that will broaden their horizons, we'll nurture interest in a new world and open up endless possibilities for the future. We value excitement towards learning and wanting to know more.
